Rafting Company

Company Purpose:

To inspire adventure and create unforgettable experiences by connecting people with nature through exhilarating whitewater rafting adventures.

Mission:

At Rafting Company, our mission is to provide safe, thrilling, and memorable rafting experiences that foster a deep appreciation for the natural world. We strive to exceed our customers' expectations by delivering exceptional service, expert guidance, and a passion for adventure.

Creed:

As stewards of the great outdoors, we believe in the transformative power of nature and the incredible bond forged through shared experiences. We are committed to upholding the highest standards of safety, professionalism, and environmental responsibility in all aspects of our operations. Our dedication to excellence drives us to continually innovate and enhance the rafting experience for adventurers of all levels.

Motto:

Unleash Your Spirit of Adventure - Explore, Thrive, Conquer!

History

Established in 2002, Rafting Company was born out of a shared passion for adventure and a love for the great outdoors. It all started when Liam Becerril, an avid outdoorsman and experienced rafting guide, envisioned creating a rafting company that would provide unforgettable experiences for adventurers of all ages and skill levels. In the early days, Liam Becerril and a small team of passionate guides set out to explore some of the most breathtaking rivers and rapids across the country. Their commitment to safety, professionalism, and environmental stewardship quickly earned them a reputation for excellence in the rafting community. As word spread about the thrilling adventures offered by Rafting Company, demand for their services grew steadily. With a focus on customer satisfaction and a dedication to delivering unforgettable experiences, the company expanded its operations, offering a wide range of rafting trips tailored to suit every adventurer's needs. Over the years, Rafting Company has continued to innovate and evolve, introducing new trips, enhancing safety measures, and investing in top-of-the-line equipment to ensure the highest quality experiences for its customers. Today, we are proud to be recognized as a leader in the rafting industry, welcoming thousands of adventurers each year to explore the wonders of whitewater rafting with us. As we look to the future, Rafting Company remains committed to our founding principles of adventure, safety, and environmental stewardship. Whether you're a first-time rafter or a seasoned thrill-seeker, we invite you to join us on the river and experience the excitement and beauty of whitewater rafting with Rafting Company.
